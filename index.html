userData: This is how Micheal recognizes you, with your information privately local there isn't a need for authentication.
  • id: User Name (Your name goes here)
  • state: Your reference to our user database.
  • mainHeading: Your political heading
  • populations: Your ideology
  • completedProjects: Badges and micro-credentials earned
